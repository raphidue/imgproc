About Us

Imgproc is the result of a team project developed by Duy Thai Nguyen and Ivan Pogorolev under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Matthias Franz in the year 2013/2014 at University of Applied Sciences.

This web application was created with Java Play Framework in order to enable all those who are interested but especially for students in image processing courses. You have the possibility to display histograms and apply filters to a image.
