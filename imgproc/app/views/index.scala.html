Upload Images

With imgproc you get the opportunity to take the first steps in image processing. First, you simply upload an image of your choice using the "Upload Image" button. Then the image is automatically converted into an 8-bit grayscale image and will be displayed on your page. Furthermore, you'll get information about the uploaded image, like the histogram of the uploaded image.

Use Filters

After you've uploaded your image, there are a variety of filters. Depending on whether the specified filter requires a filter matrix, you have the option to specify a 3x3 filter matrix. By clicking on the desired filter you apply the selected filter on your uploaded image.

Look how it works

After you are finish with experimenting by using the filter. Do you have the opportunity to learn how the filters work. You can see the function of the filters and their pseudocode on our documentation page.
