Upload Images

With imgproc you can take the first steps in image processing. First, go to the processing page. Here you can upload an image. After that it will be automatically converted into an 8-bit grayscale image and displayed on your page. On ride side you will be able to see the histogram.

Use Filters

After you've uploaded your image, you can use a variety of filters and processing operations. If the chosen filter requires a filter matrix, you have the option to specify a 3x3 filter matrix. After choosing the filter and specifying desired settings, just click on the apply button.

Look how it works

After you have finished with experimenting, you have the opportunity to learn how the filters work. You can have a look at filter descriptions and their pseudocode on the documentation page.
